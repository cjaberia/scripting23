Christine Joy Aberia

ABOUT

Christine Joy Aberia is a serial entrepreneur and a traveller, who possesses a profound passion for creativity. She is a first-year student at Lethbridge College in the Multimedia Production program focused on website design, video production and photography.

Born and raised in Manila, Philippines, CJ moved to Baguio City in her teenage years to finish her communications degree at the University of the Philippines. After this, she returned to her hometown and worked for the Marketing and Sales Department of Lufthansa Technical Training for nine years, where she discovered her love for travelling and creative technology. She has toured several countries in Asia and Europe and aspires to embark on more travel experiences to inspire her photography and video editing skills.

CJ carved a niche in the Philippines as an ardent and dedicated costume shop owner. With a profound love for cosplay and the enchanting world of make-believe, CJ was on a mission to make people's dreams a reality, one costume at a time. She shared her cosplay expertise through several television and online appearances, where her extensive array of apparel was highlighted. She also successfully ventured into food retail and e-commerce, offering diverse products to Filipino clients both offline and online.
